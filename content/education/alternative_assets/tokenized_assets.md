# Tokenized Assets

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Tokenized assets are traditional assets represented as digital tokens on a blockchain.

### Origin
Institutional adoption accelerated after 2022 with regulatory clarity.

### Why It Matters
Tokenization enables fractional ownership and programmable settlement.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Assets are custodied off-chain while tokens represent claims on-chain.

### How It Behaves
Liquidity improves for illiquid assets while regulatory risks remain.

### Key Data to Watch
- Total tokenized market capitalization
- On-chain transfer volume

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Asset managers issue tokenized funds to reduce settlement times.

### Professional Use Cases
- Tokenized money-market funds
- Fractional real estate

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Liquidity Agent**: Monitors tokenized asset flows.
- **Portfolio Agent**: Integrates tokenized yield.

### Key Takeaways
Tokenization bridges traditional finance and blockchain infrastructure.
