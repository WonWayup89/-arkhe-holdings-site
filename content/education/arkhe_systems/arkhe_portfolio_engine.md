# Arkhe Portfolio Engine

### What Is It?
The Arkhe Portfolio Engine is the autonomous system that optimizes and rebalances portfolios in real time.

### Origin
Built as the capital allocation layer of the Arkhe swarm.

### Why It Matters
Portfolio engine translates signals into optimal risk-adjusted allocations.

Portfolio engine continuously adjusts weights based on swarm consensus.
