# Arkhe Risk Engine

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
The Arkhe Risk Engine is the dedicated multi-agent system that monitors, quantifies, and mitigates portfolio risk in real time.

### Origin
Built as a core specialized agent cluster within Arkhe.

### Why It Matters
Risk-first design is fundamental to Arkhe’s institutional architecture.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
The engine aggregates signals from all agents to compute VaR, expected shortfall, and drawdown probabilities.

### How It Behaves
Risk engine overrides can reduce exposure even when other agents are bullish.

### Key Data to Watch
- Real-time drawdown probability
- Tail-risk metrics

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Arkhe Risk Engine enforces hard limits and dynamic sizing for all portfolios.

### Professional Use Cases
- Pre-trade risk validation
- Live portfolio rebalancing under stress

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Core engine for all risk calculations.
- **Supervisor Agent**: Enforces risk overrides.

### Key Takeaways
The Arkhe Risk Engine is the guardian of capital preservation within the swarm.
