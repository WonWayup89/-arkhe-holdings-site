# Arkhe Signal Scoring

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Arkhe signal scoring is the multi-agent process that assigns probabilistic confidence to market signals.

### Origin
Built as the native scoring layer of the Arkhe swarm.

### Why It Matters
Signal scoring provides calibrated probabilities rather than binary decisions.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Each agent contributes a score; swarm aggregates into a final confidence metric.

### How It Behaves
High scores require alignment across multiple specialized agents.

### Key Data to Watch
- Agent-level confidence
- Swarm consensus strength

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Arkhe signal scores are used for position sizing and risk budgeting.

### Professional Use Cases
- Conviction-weighted allocation
- Dynamic hedging triggers

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
Signal scoring is the native output format of the Arkhe swarm.

### Key Takeaways
Arkhe signal scoring transforms raw signals into institutionally usable probabilities.
