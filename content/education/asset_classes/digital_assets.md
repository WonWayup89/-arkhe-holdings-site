# Digital Assets

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Digital assets are blockchain-based tokens representing value or ownership.

### Origin
Bitcoin launched the asset class in 2009.

### Why It Matters
Digital assets provide programmable ownership.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Assets are secured by cryptography and network consensus.

### How It Behaves
Digital assets exhibit high volatility and macro correlation.

### Key Data to Watch
- On-chain activity
- ETF flows

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions allocate through regulated vehicles.

### Professional Use Cases
- Portfolio diversification
- On-chain treasury management

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Liquidity Agent**: Tracks cross-chain flows.

### Key Takeaways
Digital assets are the native instruments of blockchain networks.
