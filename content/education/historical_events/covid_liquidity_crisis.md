# COVID Liquidity Crisis

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
The March 2020 COVID liquidity crisis was a sudden global dash for cash.

### Origin
Triggered by pandemic uncertainty and forced selling.

### Why It Matters
The crisis showed how modern markets can experience near-simultaneous liquidity evaporation.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Leveraged investors and ETFs sold assets simultaneously, freezing even the Treasury market temporarily.

### How It Behaves
Central bank intervention restored liquidity faster than in 2008.

### Key Data to Watch
- Treasury bid-ask spreads
- Corporate bond liquidity metrics

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
The crisis highlighted the power of central bank backstops.

### Professional Use Cases
- Liquidity stress scenario design

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Liquidity Agent**: Uses March 2020 as benchmark liquidity stress.

### Key Takeaways
The COVID liquidity crisis was the fastest and broadest liquidity shock in modern history.
