# Regional Banking Crisis 2023

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
The 2023 regional banking crisis involved rapid failures of Silicon Valley Bank and others amid rising rates.

### Origin
Unrealized losses on long-duration securities combined with uninsured deposit runs.

### Why It Matters
The crisis showed that even well-capitalized banks can fail quickly when interest-rate risk meets liquidity risk.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Rising rates caused mark-to-market losses; social media accelerated deposit runs.

### How It Behaves
Modern banking runs occur at digital speed.

### Key Data to Watch
- Unrealized securities losses
- Uninsured deposit ratios

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
The crisis renewed focus on interest-rate risk in banking books.

### Professional Use Cases
- Bank balance-sheet stress testing

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Monitors bank duration and deposit stability.

### Key Takeaways
The 2023 crisis demonstrated persistent vulnerabilities in regional banking.
