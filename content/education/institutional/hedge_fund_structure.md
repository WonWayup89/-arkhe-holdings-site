# Hedge Fund Structure

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Hedge fund structure is the legal and fee framework that enables absolute return strategies.

### Origin
The limited partnership structure was pioneered in 1949.

### Why It Matters
Structure determines alignment of interests and operational flexibility.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Funds use limited partnerships with management and performance fees plus high-water marks.

### How It Behaves
Structures evolve to accommodate different strategies and investor types.

### Key Data to Watch
- Fee schedules
- Lock-up and redemption terms

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions negotiate side letters for enhanced terms.

### Professional Use Cases
- Master-feeder structures
- Managed accounts

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Portfolio Agent**: Models fee drag and liquidity terms.

### Key Takeaways
Hedge fund structure aligns incentives while providing flexibility for complex strategies.
