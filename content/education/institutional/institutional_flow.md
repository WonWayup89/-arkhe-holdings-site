# Institutional Flow

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Institutional flow is the aggregated buying and selling activity of large professional investors.

### Origin
Flow data became trackable after prime brokerage reporting expanded.

### Why It Matters
Institutional flows often precede sustained price trends.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Flows are measured through 13F filings, ETF data, and dark-pool prints.

### How It Behaves
Institutional buying creates self-reinforcing momentum.

### Key Data to Watch
- Quarterly 13F changes
- ETF inflows by theme

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions analyze peer flows to avoid crowded trades.

### Professional Use Cases
- Flow-based tactical overlays
- Positioning risk management

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Liquidity Agent**: Aggregates institutional flow signals.

### Key Takeaways
Institutional flow is a leading indicator of sustained market moves.
