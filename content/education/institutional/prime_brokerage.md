# Prime Brokerage

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Prime brokerage is the bundle of financing, custody, and operational services provided to hedge funds.

### Origin
The model expanded in the 1990s with hedge fund growth.

### Why It Matters
Prime brokers are the operational backbone of institutional trading.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Services include margin lending, securities lending, and consolidated reporting.

### How It Behaves
Prime brokers are profitable in normal markets but exposed to funding liquidity risk.

### Key Data to Watch
- Prime broker balances
- Securities lending revenue

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Hedge funds use multiple prime brokers to diversify counterparty risk.

### Professional Use Cases
- Portfolio financing
- Synthetic prime for digital assets

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Monitors prime broker exposure.

### Key Takeaways
Prime brokerage enables hedge funds to operate efficiently with leverage.
