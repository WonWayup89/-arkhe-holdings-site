# Quant Funds

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Quant funds rely primarily on mathematical models and automated execution.

### Origin
Renaissance Technologies pioneered modern quant investing in the 1980s.

### Why It Matters
Quant funds are a dominant force in market liquidity.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Strategies include statistical arbitrage, trend following, and machine learning.

### How It Behaves
Quant funds perform best in normal regimes and can suffer simultaneous losses during crowded trades.

### Key Data to Watch
- Assets under management in systematic strategies
- Factor crowding metrics

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Leading quant funds operate massive compute clusters.

### Professional Use Cases
- Multi-strategy quant platforms
- Systematic trend programs

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **ML Agent**: Evolves quantitative signals.

### Key Takeaways
Quant funds industrialize investing through data-driven models.
