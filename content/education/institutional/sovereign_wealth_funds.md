# Sovereign Wealth Funds

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Sovereign wealth funds are state-owned investment vehicles managing national savings or commodity revenues.

### Origin
The first modern funds were established in the 1950s.

### Why It Matters
Sovereign wealth funds are among the largest patient capital pools globally.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Funds follow mandates ranging from stabilization to return generation.

### How It Behaves
Investment horizons are multi-decade.

### Key Data to Watch
- Total assets under management
- Allocation to alternatives

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Funds pursue direct investments and strategic stakes.

### Professional Use Cases
- Strategic infrastructure investments
- Diversification from commodity dependence

### Key Takeaways
Sovereign wealth funds shape global capital flows and strategic industries.
