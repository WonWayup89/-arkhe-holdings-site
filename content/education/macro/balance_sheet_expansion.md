# Balance Sheet Expansion

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Balance sheet expansion is the increase in central bank assets through asset purchases.

### Origin
Became the primary tool during the 2008 crisis.

### Why It Matters
Balance sheet expansion is the main channel of unconventional monetary policy.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Central banks purchase securities, expanding reserves and lowering yields.

### How It Behaves
Expansion compresses risk premia and supports asset prices.

### Key Data to Watch
- Central bank balance sheet size

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions position for the liquidity effects of balance sheet expansion.

### Professional Use Cases
- Liquidity-driven risk-on positioning

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Macro Agent**: Tracks balance sheet effects on liquidity.

### Key Takeaways
Balance sheet expansion is the central bank’s primary liquidity tool.
