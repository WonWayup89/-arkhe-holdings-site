# Fiscal Policy

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Fiscal policy is government spending and taxation used to influence economic activity.

### Origin
Modern fiscal policy was shaped by Keynes in the 1930s.

### Why It Matters
Fiscal policy can provide powerful stimulus independent of monetary policy.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Expansionary fiscal policy increases deficits; contractionary policy reduces them.

### How It Behaves
Fiscal policy is most effective when monetary policy is accommodative.

### Key Data to Watch
- Budget deficit
- Fiscal impulse

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions position for fiscal expansions through risk assets.

### Professional Use Cases
- Fiscal stimulus trade implementation

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Macro Agent**: Incorporates fiscal impulse into forecasts.

### Key Takeaways
Fiscal policy is a powerful but politically constrained tool.
