# Interest Rates

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Interest rates are the cost of borrowing money or the return on lending.

### Origin
Central banks set policy rates as the primary monetary policy tool.

### Why It Matters
Interest rates are the most important price in the economy.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Policy rates influence the entire yield curve and asset valuations.

### How It Behaves
Rate changes transmit through borrowing costs, asset prices, and currency values.

### Key Data to Watch
- Policy rate and dot plot
- Yield curve shape

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions position portfolios according to expected rate paths.

### Professional Use Cases
- Rate path forecasting

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Macro Agent**: Models central bank reaction functions.

### Key Takeaways
Interest rates are the master variable of monetary policy transmission.
