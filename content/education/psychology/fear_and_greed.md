# Fear and Greed

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Fear and greed are the dominant emotions driving investor behavior and price deviations from fundamentals.

### Origin
Recognized for centuries and popularized in modern finance.

### Why It Matters
Fear and greed create exploitable market inefficiencies.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Fear causes risk aversion and selling pressure; greed causes risk-seeking and leverage expansion.

### How It Behaves
Extreme fear creates capitulation; extreme greed creates euphoria.

### Key Data to Watch
- Sentiment indices
- Put/call ratios

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Professionals use fear and greed as contrarian signals.

### Professional Use Cases
- Sentiment-based tactical overlays

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Sentiment Agent**: Quantifies fear and greed.

### Key Takeaways
Fear and greed are the emotional engines of market cycles.
