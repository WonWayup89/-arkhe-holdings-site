# FOMO

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
FOMO is the anxiety of missing profitable opportunities that others are capturing.

### Origin
Widely used in finance during late-stage bull markets.

### Why It Matters
FOMO drives late-cycle buying and bubble formation.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
FOMO is visible in accelerating inflows and narrative-driven price moves.

### How It Behaves
FOMO peaks near tops and is followed by sharp reversals.

### Key Data to Watch
- Retail inflows at accelerating rates
- Margin debt levels

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Professionals reduce exposure as FOMO intensifies.

### Professional Use Cases
- Tactical de-risking

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Sentiment Agent**: Detects accelerating FOMO.

### Key Takeaways
FOMO is a reliable precursor to major reversals.
