# Panic Selling

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Panic selling is the rapid liquidation of assets driven by fear regardless of fundamentals.

### Origin
Observed in every major market decline.

### Why It Matters
Panic selling creates capitulation and often marks market bottoms.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Panic is accelerated by margin calls, forced redemptions, and social media.

### How It Behaves
Panic exhausts selling pressure and sets the stage for recovery.

### Key Data to Watch
- Spike in trading volume during declines
- Sentiment survey lows

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Contrarian investors increase exposure during panic phases.

### Professional Use Cases
- Opportunistic buying

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Sentiment Agent**: Identifies panic through volume-sentiment divergence.

### Key Takeaways
Panic selling frequently coincides with the best long-term entry points.
