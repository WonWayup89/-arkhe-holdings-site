# Reflexivity

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Reflexivity is the feedback loop where perceptions influence fundamentals and vice versa.

### Origin

### Why It Matters
Reflexivity explains why markets deviate far from equilibrium.

Reflexive processes create prolonged trends followed by violent reversals.
