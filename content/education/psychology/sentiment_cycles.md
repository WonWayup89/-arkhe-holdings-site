# Sentiment Cycles

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Sentiment cycles are the recurring waves of investor optimism and pessimism that drive market phases.

### Origin
Observed in every major market cycle.

### Why It Matters
Sentiment cycles create predictable opportunities for contrarian positioning.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Sentiment moves from skepticism to optimism, euphoria, anxiety, fear, and capitulation.

### How It Behaves
Extreme sentiment often coincides with valuation extremes.

### Key Data to Watch
- Sentiment survey extremes
- Media tone uniformity

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Professionals use sentiment cycles as tactical overlays.

### Professional Use Cases
- Contrarian positioning at extremes

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Sentiment Agent**: Maps current sentiment cycle stage.

### Key Takeaways
Sentiment cycles are the emotional counterpart to economic cycles.
