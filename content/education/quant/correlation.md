# Correlation

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Correlation measures the degree to which two assets move together.

### Origin
Correlation became central to portfolio theory after Markowitz in 1952.

### Why It Matters
Correlation determines diversification benefits.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Correlation ranges from -1 to +1. Dynamic correlation matrices are used for portfolio optimization.

### How It Behaves
Correlations spike during crises, reducing diversification.

### Key Data to Watch
- Pairwise correlation matrix
- Average cross-asset correlation

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions monitor correlation risk and maintain explicit hedges.

### Professional Use Cases
- Correlation swap trading
- Regime-aware portfolio construction

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Detects rising correlations.

### Key Takeaways
Correlation is the key determinant of portfolio diversification.
