# GARCH Models

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
GARCH models forecast time-varying volatility.

### Origin
Introduced by Bollerslev in 1986.

### Why It Matters
GARCH captures volatility clustering essential for risk management.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Variance is modeled as a function of past variances and squared returns.

### How It Behaves
GARCH forecasts mean-revert to long-term variance.

### Key Data to Watch
- GARCH parameters
- Forecasted versus realized volatility

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Risk departments use GARCH for VaR and option pricing.

### Professional Use Cases
- Dynamic volatility forecasting
- Risk-neutral density estimation

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Incorporates GARCH forecasts.

### Key Takeaways
GARCH is the workhorse of volatility forecasting.
