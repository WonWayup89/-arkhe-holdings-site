# Statistical Edge

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
A statistical edge is a repeatable positive expected-value pattern in market data.

### Origin
Central to quantitative trading since the 1980s.

### Why It Matters
A sustainable statistical edge is the foundation of profitable systematic strategies.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Edges are identified through statistical testing and walk-forward validation.

### How It Behaves
Most edges decay over time.

### Key Data to Watch
- Out-of-sample Sharpe ratio
- Signal decay rate

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Quant funds maintain research pipelines for edge discovery and retirement.

### Professional Use Cases
- Alpha signal production
- Multi-signal ensembles

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **ML Agent**: Hunts for new edges and retires decaying ones.

### Key Takeaways
A statistical edge must be rigorously validated and defended against decay.
