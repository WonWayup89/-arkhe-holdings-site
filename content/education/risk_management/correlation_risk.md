# Correlation Risk

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Correlation risk is the risk that historical asset relationships break down during stress.

### Origin
2008 highlighted how correlations spike in crises.

### Why It Matters
Diversification fails when it is needed most.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Correlations are regime-dependent and spike during liquidity shocks.

### How It Behaves
Correlation risk amplifies portfolio drawdowns.

### Key Data to Watch
- Dynamic correlation matrices

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions maintain explicit hedges for correlation breakdowns.

### Professional Use Cases
- Correlation swap trading

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Detects rising cross-asset correlations.

### Key Takeaways
Correlation risk is the silent killer of diversification.
