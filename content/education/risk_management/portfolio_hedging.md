# Portfolio Hedging

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Portfolio hedging uses derivatives to reduce adverse price risk.

### Origin
Standard after option pricing models in the 1970s.

### Why It Matters
Hedging allows maintenance of core exposures while limiting downside.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Common tools include index futures and options.

### How It Behaves
Hedges reduce volatility but can cap upside.

### Key Data to Watch
- Hedge ratio effectiveness

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions run dynamic hedging programs.

### Professional Use Cases
- Equity tail-risk hedging

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Optimizes hedge ratios.

### Key Takeaways
Portfolio hedging balances protection with cost.
