# Stress Testing

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Stress testing evaluates portfolio performance under extreme adverse scenarios.

### Origin
Mandatory after the 2008 crisis.

### Why It Matters
Stress tests reveal vulnerabilities beyond normal risk metrics.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Tests include historical and hypothetical scenarios with second-order effects.

### How It Behaves
Effective stress testing incorporates liquidity spirals and correlation breakdowns.

### Key Data to Watch
- Portfolio loss under 2008-level shock

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions run daily internal stress tests.

### Professional Use Cases
- Pre-trade stress limits

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Risk Agent**: Generates stress scenarios.

### Key Takeaways
Stress testing identifies risks that lie beyond statistical measures.
