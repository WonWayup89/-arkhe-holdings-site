# Carry Trade

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
A carry trade borrows in a low-yielding asset and invests in a higher-yielding one.

### Origin
Popular after the end of fixed exchange rates.

### Why It Matters
Carry trades are a core source of return in currency and fixed-income markets.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
The trade earns the interest differential but is exposed to sudden unwinds.

### How It Behaves
Carry trades perform steadily in calm markets and suffer sharp losses in risk-off events.

### Key Data to Watch
- Interest-rate differentials
- Funding liquidity conditions

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Global macro funds run large carry books with dynamic hedging.

### Professional Use Cases
- Currency carry portfolios
- Volatility carry

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Macro Agent**: Monitors carry attractiveness.

### Key Takeaways
Carry trades harvest risk premia but require careful risk management.
