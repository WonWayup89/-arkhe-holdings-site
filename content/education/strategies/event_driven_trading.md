# Event Driven Trading

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Event-driven trading profits from corporate events such as mergers and earnings.

### Origin
Evolved from merger arbitrage in the 1980s.

### Why It Matters
Event-driven strategies are largely independent of market direction.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Traders analyze deal terms and probabilities.

### How It Behaves
Returns are driven by event resolution.

### Key Data to Watch
- Deal spread
- Implied probability of completion

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Multi-strategy funds maintain dedicated event-driven books.

### Professional Use Cases
- Merger arbitrage
- Special situations

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Research Agent**: Analyzes event probability.

### Key Takeaways
Event-driven trading isolates idiosyncratic catalysts.
