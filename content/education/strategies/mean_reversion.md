# Mean Reversion

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Mean reversion bets that prices will revert to their historical average.

### Origin
Formalized in statistical arbitrage in the 1980s.

### Why It Matters
Mean reversion exploits temporary mispricings.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Models use z-scores or cointegration to enter positions.

### How It Behaves
The strategy performs best in range-bound markets.

### Key Data to Watch
- Deviation from mean
- Half-life of reversion

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Quant funds run high-frequency mean-reversion books.

### Professional Use Cases
- Pairs trading
- Volatility mean reversion

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **ML Agent**: Detects regime shifts.

### Key Takeaways
Mean reversion profits from temporary dislocations.
