# Momentum Trading

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Momentum trading buys assets that have recently performed well and sells those that have performed poorly.

### Origin
Documented academically in 1993.

### Why It Matters
Momentum is one of the most persistent anomalies.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Models rank securities by past returns and rebalance.

### How It Behaves
Momentum works best in trending markets and crashes during reversals.

### Key Data to Watch
- Momentum factor returns
- Crash risk indicators

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Institutions implement momentum through ETFs and overlays.

### Professional Use Cases
- Cross-asset momentum programs
- Sector rotation

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Technical Agent**: Generates momentum signals.

### Key Takeaways
Momentum systematically exploits the tendency of winners to continue winning.
