# Scalping

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Scalping profits from very small price changes through high-frequency trades.

### Origin
Viable after electronic trading in the late 1990s.

### Why It Matters
Scalping demonstrates the importance of speed and execution.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Scalpers rely on level-2 quotes and order-flow analysis.

### How It Behaves
The strategy requires low-latency infrastructure.

### Key Data to Watch
- Average profit per trade
- Trade frequency

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Proprietary firms operate sophisticated scalping strategies.

### Professional Use Cases
- Market-making scalping
- Order-flow scalping

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Execution Agent**: Optimizes micro-level timing.

### Key Takeaways
Scalping is the highest-frequency expression of market making.
