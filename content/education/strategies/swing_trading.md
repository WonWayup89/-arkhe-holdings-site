# Swing Trading

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Swing trading holds positions for days to weeks to capture short- to medium-term price swings.

### Origin
Popularized with electronic brokerage in the 1990s.

### Why It Matters
Swing trading balances frequency and patience.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Traders use technical patterns and momentum indicators.

### How It Behaves
Performance depends on high-probability setup identification.

### Key Data to Watch
- Average holding period
- Win rate and reward-to-risk

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Systematic swing strategies are automated by quant groups.

### Professional Use Cases
- Technical swing programs
- Event-driven swing trading

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Technical Agent**: Identifies swing setups.

### Key Takeaways
Swing trading captures meaningful price movements with disciplined risk control.
