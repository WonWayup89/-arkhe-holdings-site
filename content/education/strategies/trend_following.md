# Trend Following

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Trend following identifies and rides sustained price movements.

### Origin
Formalized in the 1970s by commodity trading advisors.

### Why It Matters
Trend following provides crisis alpha and diversification.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Models use moving averages or breakouts with volatility targeting.

### How It Behaves
The strategy performs best during prolonged directional markets.

### Key Data to Watch
- Trend strength metrics
- Drawdown duration

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Large CTAs run multi-asset trend-following programs.

### Professional Use Cases
- Managed futures programs
- Crisis risk mitigation

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Technical Agent**: Identifies trend signals.

### Key Takeaways
Trend following profits from market persistence.
