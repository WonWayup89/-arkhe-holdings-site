# Volatility Trading

## Beginner Level

### What Is It?
Volatility trading seeks to profit from changes in implied volatility.

### Origin
Institutionalized after the VIX and listed volatility products.

### Why It Matters
Volatility is a distinct asset class with mean-reverting properties.

## Intermediate Level

### Market Mechanics
Traders use options and variance swaps to take views on realized versus implied volatility.

### How It Behaves
Volatility spikes during crises and mean-reverts during calm periods.

### Key Data to Watch
- Implied versus realized volatility
- Volatility term structure

## Advanced Level

### Institutional Behavior
Dedicated volatility funds trade volatility as a standalone asset class.

### Professional Use Cases
- Volatility arbitrage
- Tail-risk hedging

### AI Interpretation in Systems Like Arkhe
- **Technical Agent**: Forecasts volatility regimes.

### Key Takeaways
Volatility trading treats implied volatility as a tradable asset.
